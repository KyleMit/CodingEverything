## Questions

* Don't center Text

  Settings > Editor > Disable "Readable Line Length"

* [Line numbers in edit mode](https://forum.obsidian.md/t/line-numbers-in-edit-mode-core-function-or-css-theme/21403)

  Settings > Editor > Show Line Number
